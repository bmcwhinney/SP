What is a closed group workshop?

Unlike open workshops where participants may change from session to session, a closed group brings together the same small group of people over a series of meetings. This creates deeper trust, continuity, and the opportunity to explore themes with increasing depth over time.

Each session follows the same constellation process used in open workshops – issue holders bring personal or professional themes to explore, while others participate as representatives.
