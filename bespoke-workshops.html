Exploring a Theme, Question, or Issue Together

Sometimes a group comes together to explore something meaningful: a shared question, a challenge, or a theme that feels alive. These bespoke Systemic and Family Constellations workshops support that kind of inquiry, whether the group already exists or is formed specifically for the work.

A workshop can be arranged for an existing group, team, or community, or an individual within a company or organisation can commission a workshop, in which case Sara can gather an external group to participate.

Each workshop is shaped around the theme or question the group brings. For example:

Honouring Our Ancestors, Envisioning the Past

For the 50th anniversary of the Association of Family Therapy (AFT), a group explored this theme through a systemic constellations lens.

Our Relationship with Money

The Bitcoin community explored personal and collective relationships with money.

These examples show the range of inquiry possible, but each workshop is tailored to the group and the question they bring.

How a Bespoke Workshop Works

Workshops are guided by Sara in a safe, supportive, and confidential space. The group may already exist, or it may be gathered specifically for the constellation.

During the workshop:

  • Issue Holders bring a personal or shared question.
  • Representatives take part in the constellation, creating a living map of the system.

As the constellation unfolds, hidden patterns and dynamics often become visible. The work is not about quick fixes, but about creating movement, opening new viewpoints, and generating fresh possibilities.

No prior experience with Systemic or Family Constellations is required. Workshops are offered in person in the UK and Europe.
