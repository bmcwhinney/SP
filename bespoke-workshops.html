Exploring a Theme, Question, or Issue Together

Bespoke Systemic and Family Constellations workshops support the exploration of meaningful personal or professional questions and challenges.

These workshops can be designed for existing groups, teams, or communities, or commissioned by an individual or organisation. In commissioned workshops, participants can be brought together specifically for the session.

Each workshop is shaped around the theme or question the group brings, helping participants uncover patterns, gain insights, and make practical shifts.

How a Bespoke Workshop Works

Workshops are guided by Sara in a safe, supportive, and confidential space. The group may already exist, or it may be gathered specifically for the constellation.

During the workshop:

  • Issue Holders bring a personal or shared question.
  • Representatives take part in the constellation, creating a living map of the system.

As the constellation unfolds, hidden patterns and dynamics often become visible. The work is not about quick fixes, but about creating movement, opening new viewpoints, and generating fresh possibilities.

No prior experience with Systemic or Family Constellations is required. Workshops are offered in person in the UK and Europe.

About Sara

Sara is a systemic therapist with over ten years' experience facilitating Family Constellations workshops. She brings warmth, presence, and clarity to every session, creating a space where participants feel safe to explore, reflect, and discover.

Constellations are a process for expanding understanding and awareness. They are not a diagnostic or treatment method for any psychological condition or disorder.
